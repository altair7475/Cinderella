Welcome to Cinderella

At Cinderella, we believe every plushie has the power to bring comfort, spark imagination, and create lasting memories. From bedtime companions to thoughtful gifts, our plush friends are designed to make people of all ages smile.

Our mission is to create high-quality, thoughtfully designed plush toys that inspire joy, comfort, and connection.
