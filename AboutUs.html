About Us Welcome to Cinderella At Cinderella., we believe every plushie has the power to bring comfort, spark imagination, and create lasting memories. From bedtime companions to thoughtful gifts, our plush friends are designed to make people of all ages smile. Our Mission Our mission is to create high-quality, thoughtfully designed plush toys that inspire joy, comfort, and connection. We are committed to using soft, durable materials and crafting lovable characters that become lifelong companions for families around the world. Our Vision We envision a world where everyone has a source of comfort they can hold close. Our goal is to become a trusted name in plush toys by combining creativity, quality, and sustainability while bringing happiness to every home we reach. Our Story Cinderella. was founded in 2021 by three lifelong friends who shared a passion for design, storytelling, and collecting plush toys. What began as a small online shop selling handmade stuffed animals quickly grew into a thriving brand thanks to the support of a growing community of plush enthusiasts. Inspired by childhood memories and the comfort a favorite toy can provide, the founders set out to create plushies with unique personalities and charming backstories. Today, Cozy Critter Co. offers a wide collection of cuddly companions, from classic teddy bears to whimsical fantasy creatures, while staying true to the values that inspired its beginnings. Meet Our Team Our team is made up of designers, artists, product developers, and customer care specialists who all share one goal: creating plush toys that people genuinely love. Haysab – Founder & Creative Director Emma leads the design team and oversees the creation of every new plush character, ensuring each one has its own unique personality. Reyner – Co-Founder & Operations Manager Noah manages production, quality assurance, and logistics, making sure every plushie meets our high standards before reaching customers. Bosco – Head of Customer Experience Sophia and her team are dedicated to providing friendly support and ensuring every customer enjoys a smooth shopping experience.
